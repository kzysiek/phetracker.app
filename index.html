PheTracker Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

1. Overview

PheTracker is a mobile application designed to help families manage phenylketonuria (PKU) through daily tracking of phenylalanine (Phe) intake.

2. Data We Collect

  • User account data (authentication via Microsoft Entra)
  • Child profiles (name, optional birth date, dietary limits)
  • Food diary entries and nutritional data
  • Scanned data (barcode and nutrition label OCR)

3. Camera Usage

The app uses your device camera to:

  • Scan product barcodes
  • Scan nutrition labels (OCR)

The camera is only used when you explicitly activate scanning. No images are recorded or stored without your action.

4. How We Use Data

Your data is used to:

  • Calculate phenylalanine (Phe) intake
  • Generate reports
  • Provide core app functionality

5. Data Storage

Data is stored securely using cloud infrastructure (Microsoft Azure).

6. Third-Party Services

  • Microsoft Entra – authentication
  • RevenueCat – subscription management

These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

7. User Rights

You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

8. Contact

Email: support@phetracker.app
